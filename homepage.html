Trust Architecture

Some truths remain sacred.

CommonUnity is built around a simple conviction: not everything should be transmitted, analysed, or folded into a model. Sacred Mode is a boundary — a contemplative trust layer for material that belongs only to you. When you enter it, the surface changes. The periphery softens. The writing field becomes a held space. AI does not enter. What you write there stays there. This is not a privacy setting. It is a design philosophy.
