Eight Limbs · Digital Governance

A framework for conscious technology.

The Eight Limbs of yoga are not only a personal practice framework. Inside CommonUnity, they become the governing principles of how technology is built and how AI is used.

i Yama

Ahimsa applies to architecture. The system does not harm, extract, or manipulate.

ii Niyama

Inner discipline. Every feature asks: does this support wholeness?

iii Asana

The steady and easeful seat. AI relationship should not strain.

iv Pranayama

Rhythm and pause. Sometimes the correct response is space.

v Pratyahara

Withdrawal. Sacred Mode: contemplate without offering to the machine.

vi Dharana

Concentration. AI protects focus rather than fragments it.

vii Dhyana

Sustained flow. AI carries complexity so depth remains intact.

viii Samadhi

Union. AI serves the conditions around it. It does not claim this space.

OM Cipher

Your source pattern, sealed.

The OM Cipher is your foundational identity record inside CommonUnity — derived from your birth data, Gene Keys, Human Design, name resonance, seed syllable, and the frequency of your own voice, captured through Bhramari pranayama. It is the first thing you build here, and it belongs entirely to you.

It is not an avatar. Not a badge. It is the geometry of your given structure — compressed, deterministic, yours. It is the seal of your initiation into CommonUnity, and the private foundation beneath everything that follows.

Your Cipher is sealed by default. You choose what, if anything, is shared.

Nexus

Intelligence in service of practice.

Nexus is the AI synthesis layer inside CommonUnity. It holds the full context of your cOMpass, your reflections, and your session — and thinks with you, not for you. It is the most advanced part of the system: genuinely contextual, genuinely responsive to who you are rather than to a generic prompt.

It is not a chatbot. It begins from listening — from your patterns, your current work, your living profile — and moves through resonance toward coherence. It does not replace discernment, embodiment, relationship, or lived experience. It is technology designed to strengthen the human core: attention, intuition, responsibility, creative action. Co-emergence, not automation.

Begin from listening. Move through resonance. Return toward coherence.

Trust Architecture

Some truths remain sacred.

Sacred Mode is a writing space inside cOMpass and stUdio where AI does not enter. What you write there stays there — unindexed, unanalysed, unshared. It exists because CommonUnity is built on a simple conviction: not everything should be transmitted, or folded into a model.

When you enter it, the surface changes. The periphery softens. The writing field becomes a held space. It teaches a kind of discernment — about what is true, what is necessary, and what is yours alone to carry. This is not a privacy setting. It is a design philosophy.
